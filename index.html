About Our Church

For over 50 years, Grace Community Church has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community. We are a diverse congregation united by our love for God and commitment to serving others.

Our mission is to create a welcoming environment where people can grow in their faith, build meaningful relationships, and discover their purpose in God's plan. We believe in the transformative power of love, grace, and community.

Whether you're new to faith or have been walking with God for years, you'll find a home here at Grace Community Church. Come as you are, and let's journey together in faith.
